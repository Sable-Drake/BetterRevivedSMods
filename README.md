# Better Revived S-Mods

This mod enhances hullmods when they are S-Modded (built in with Story Points), making them more deserving of the investment. Updated to leverage the modern vanilla S-Mod system.

## S-Mod Bonuses by Hullmod

### Accelerated Shield Emitter
- +400% additional shield turn rate (total: +500%)
- +400% additional shield unfold rate (total: +500%)

### Adaptive Phase Coils
- +25% top speed and acceleration while phase cloak active
- -25% phase cloak cooldown

### Additional Berthing
- Doubles crew capacity increase
- Negates maintenance cost increase for civilian hulls

### Advanced Optics
- Converts the +200 flat range bonus to a base range increase
- Percentage and multiplicative modifiers (like Integrated Targeting Unit, skills) apply to the increased base value

### Advanced Turret Gyros
- +25% damage to missiles and fighters
- +5% damage per size class difference to smaller ships (Capital: +20% to frigates, +10% to destroyers, +5% to cruisers)

### Armored Weapons
- +10% rate of fire for ballistic and energy weapons

### Augmented Engines
- +1 additional burn level
- -30% fuel use

### Automated Repair Unit
- -75% repair time (improved from -50%)
- 15% of hull and armor damage repaired after combat at no cost
- -15% CR loss from deployment
- -25% overload duration

### Auxiliary Fuel Tanks
- Doubles fuel capacity increase
- Negates maintenance cost increase for civilian hulls

### Auxiliary Thrusters
- Increases maneuverability bonus to 66% (from 50%)
- Applies to acceleration, deceleration, turn acceleration, and turn rate
- +10 zero-flux speed boost
- Doubles zero-flux turn rate bonus

### Ballistic Rangefinder
- Same base range increase (already increases base range, so scales with modifiers)

### Blast Doors
- -85% total crew casualties (60% base + 25% S-Mod)
- -25% high explosive damage taken by hull and armor
- -10% armor damage taken

### Converted Fighter Bay
- -10% monthly maintenance cost per bay

### Converted Hangar
- +10% fighter replacement rate recovery (Cruiser)
- +25% fighter replacement rate recovery (Capital)
- Bomber OP cost penalty reduced

### Dedicated Targeting Core
- +40% weapon range (Cruiser)
- +60% weapon range (Capital)

### ECCM Package
- 100% ECCM chance (fully negates ECM/flares)
- 0% EW penalty (fully negates EW penalty)

### ECM Package
- +1% additional ECM rating (total: 2%/3%/4%/5%)
- Ship and fighters gain +50% target leading accuracy

### Efficiency Overhaul
- +20% maximum combat readiness
- -50% maintenance costs for civilian hulls (improved from -20%)
- +100% CR recovery and repair rate for combat ships (improved from +50%)

### Expanded Cargo Holds
- Doubles cargo capacity increase
- Negates maintenance cost increase for civilian hulls

### Expanded Deck Crew
- +25% faster fighter replacement rate recovery

### Expanded Magazines
- +50% ballistic ammo regen rate
- +50% energy ammo regen rate

### Extended Shield Emitter
- +60 additional degrees shield arc (total: +120)
- If shield arc exceeds 360 degrees: Increases flux capacity scaling with shield upkeep (up to 12x upkeep cost)

### Faulty Automated Systems (D-Mod)
- None (D-Mods don't receive S-Mod bonuses)

### Flux Breakers
- +33% flux dissipation while venting (improved from +25%)
- -20% overload duration

### Flux Coil Adjunct
- +400/+800/+1200/+2000 additional flux capacity (Frigate/Destroyer/Cruiser/Capital)
- When overloaded: +100% flux dissipation (negates overload penalty)

### Flux Distributor
- +15/+30/+40/+50 additional flux dissipation (Frigate/Destroyer/Cruiser/Capital)
- -5% flux generated by energy, ballistic, and missile weapons

### Front Shield Emitter
- +25% additional shield arc (total: +125%)
- -75% shield upkeep (improved from -50%)
- +5% shield damage reduction

### Front Shield Generator
- Shield becomes omni-directional with -20% upkeep and +10% efficiency
- No top speed reduction

### Hardened Shield Emitter
- -22% shield damage taken (improved from -20%)

### Hardened Subsystems
- +30 seconds peak operating time
- -50% chance for weapon, engine, and critical malfunctions

### Heavy Armor
- -25% maneuverability (increased penalty from -10%)
- +20% armor

### High Resolution Sensors
- +1000/+1500/+2000/+2500 sight radius (Frigate/Destroyer/Cruiser/Capital)

### High Scatter Amplifier
- +10% additional beam damage (total: +20%)
- Range penalty reduced to 0.75x (improved from 0.5x)

### Insulated Engines
- +100% additional engine health (total: +200%)
- -75% sensor profile (improved from -50%)

### Integrated Point Defense AI
- +50% damage to fighters
- +100 flat range to all PD weapons

### Militarized Subsystems
- +15% flux dissipation (improved from +10%)
- +20 flat armor (improved from +10)
- +25% damage to fighters and missiles
- +30 flat range to PD weapons
- +20% maneuverability (acceleration, deceleration, turn rate)
- +10% hull, armor, and flux capacity

### Nav Relay
- Fleet nav rating also increases this ship's maneuverability (acceleration, deceleration, turn rate)
- Fighters gain +20% top speed and maneuverability

### Omni Shield Emitter
- Removes the -30% shield arc penalty

### Operations Center
- If flagship or neurally linked: +5% ECM rating, +5% Nav rating, retains command point recovery
- If flagship: Deployment point cost and supply costs reduced by 15% or 10 points (whichever is less)

### Recovery Shuttles
- -90% fighter pilot casualties (improved from -75%)
- -40% fighter replacement rate loss when engaging

### Reinforced Bulkheads
- +250/+400/+500/+1000 additional hull integrity (Frigate/Destroyer/Cruiser/Capital)
- -50% negative effects from d-mods

### Rugged Construction
- None (no additional S-Mod bonus)

### Shield Shunt
- +15% additional armor (total: +30%)

### Solar Shielding
- -33% energy damage taken (improved from -20%)

### Stabilized Shield Emitter
- +12% hard flux dissipation while shields are active

### Unstable Injector
- +25/+20/+15/+15 zero-flux speed boost (Frigate/Destroyer/Cruiser/Capital)
